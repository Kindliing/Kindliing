You must enable JavaScript to use Kindliing properly!

Q: Why do I need to enable JavaScript? That makes my Kindle lag out.

A: JavaScript is needed for Kindliing to search things and insert styling.

Q: Alright, but how do I enable it?

A: Tap the triple dots next to the URL bar and then tap "Browser Settings." Then tap "Enable JavaScript."
